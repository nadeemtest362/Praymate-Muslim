# Personal Prayers App - Go-to-Market Strategy

## Executive Summary

Launch Personal Prayers as the premier Christian prayer app focusing on consistency, community, and spiritual growth.

## Target Market

- Primary: Daily practicing Christians (ages 25-45)
- Secondary: Church communities and youth groups
- Tertiary: New believers seeking prayer guidance

## 6-Week Launch Plan

### Week 1: Foundation & Research

**Strategic Planning**

- Define clear value proposition
- Identify target user personas
- Competitive analysis (Hallow, Pray.com, Abide)
- Set success metrics and KPIs

**Market Research**

- User interviews (minimum 50)
- Survey potential users
- Analyze competitor features
- Document pain points

### Week 2: Product & Brand

**Development**

- Landing page with email capture
- Analytics implementation
- Beta signup flow
- A/B testing framework

**Design**

- Brand identity creation
- Landing page design
- App store assets
- Onboarding flow mockups

### Week 3: Content Strategy

**Content Creation**

- Blog post series on prayer habits
- Social media content calendar
- Email nurture sequence
- App store optimization copy

**Community Building**

- Identify key Christian influencers
- Partner with churches
- Create ambassador program
- Build Facebook group

### Week 4: Pre-Launch Preparation

**Technical Setup**

- Push notification system
- Referral tracking
- Analytics dashboards
- Load testing

**Marketing Assets**

- Video testimonials
- Social proof collection
- Press kit preparation
- Ad creative variations

### Week 5: Launch Week

**Launch Execution**

- Coordinated social media blast
- Email to beta list
- PR outreach
- Community announcements

**Growth Hacking**

- Referral program activation
- Early bird pricing
- Streak challenges
- Social sharing incentives

### Week 6: Optimization

**Data Analysis**

- User behavior analysis
- Conversion funnel optimization
- Feature usage tracking
- Churn analysis

**Iteration**

- Onboarding improvements
- Feature prioritization
- Bug fixes and polish
- Content strategy refinement

## Key Success Metrics

- Week 1: 1,000 email signups
- Week 5: 5,000 app downloads
- Week 6: 30% 7-day retention
- Month 3: 25,000 MAU

## Budget Allocation

- Development: 40%
- Marketing: 35%
- Design: 15%
- Operations: 10%
